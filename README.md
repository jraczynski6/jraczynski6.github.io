# Practice 1: Portfolio MVP

## Instructions

For this assignment, you will create an MVP of your portfolio page that includes:

### Home Page
- Images that express your personal brand.
- A brief summary of who you are, what you can do (include your new technical skills!), and what you are seeking for your career.

### Resume and Achievements
- MVP: Provide a downloadable link to your resume.
- Include a visual representation of your resume (PDF, image, or embedded preview).

### Project Work
- MVP: Include the name and description of at least one project.
- Add at least one screenshot.
- Include existing links to your front-end project(s), such as GitHub repository links.

### Contact
- Provide an email address and/or LinkedIn link so visitors can reach you.
